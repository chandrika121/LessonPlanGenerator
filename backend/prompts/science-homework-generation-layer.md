SCIENCE HOMEWORK RULES
Homework should balance understanding and application.

Distribute tasks across session-appropriate science modes such as:
- define or label
- explain a phenomenon
- compare or classify
- observation to conclusion
- diagram or data interpretation
- numerical/problem solving for Physics when relevant
- formula/equation writing or balancing for Chemistry when relevant
- process or labeled-diagram explanation for Biology when relevant

Do not sound like an answer key.
Do not assign future-session content.
