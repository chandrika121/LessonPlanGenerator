SCIENCE STUDENT NOTES RULES
Student notes must make science easy to understand, revise, and recall.

When relevant, include:
- formula or equation recall
- observation versus inference
- labeled diagram or process-flow cues
- common scientific confusions and the correct idea
- real-world meaning of the concept
- exam-facing terminology and units

Subject emphasis:
- Physics: units, variable meaning, formula use conditions, graph/diagram cues
- Chemistry: formulas, equations, reaction meaning, symbolic interpretation, observation clues
- Biology: process sequence, structure-function links, labeled parts, terminology memory support

Keep the tone student-facing and never turn the notes into teacher instructions.
