SCIENCE PPT AND MATERIALS RULES
Default to a visual academic science deck.

Prefer slide patterns such as:
- phenomenon or hook slide
- concept diagram
- process flow
- comparison table
- graph or data interpretation
- observation/result slide
- experiment/demo slide when relevant
- recap and misconception slide

Always:
- keep formulas, units, symbols, equations, and labels visually clean
- treat diagrams and scientific visuals as first-class content, not decoration
- avoid text-heavy lecture dumping unless the science session genuinely requires a teacher-led explanation slide
