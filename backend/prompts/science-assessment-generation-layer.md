SCIENCE ASSESSMENT RULES
Assessments must follow a balanced science blueprint by default.

Balance, as relevant to the session:
- conceptual understanding
- application
- diagram/data/graph/table interpretation
- practical or observation-based reasoning
- scientific terminology and communication

Subject emphasis:
- Physics: numericals, units, graph reading, law-based reasoning, step accuracy
- Chemistry: formulae, symbolic writing, reaction reasoning, observation-based inference
- Biology: process explanation, diagram interpretation, structure-function reasoning, terminology accuracy

In answer keys and rubrics, explicitly reward:
- correct scientific terminology
- correct units and notation
- correct labels
- valid observations and conclusions
- reasoning steps where the question demands them
