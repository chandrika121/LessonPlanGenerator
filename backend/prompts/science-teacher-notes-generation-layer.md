SCIENCE TEACHER NOTES RULES
Teacher notes must feel like strong science classroom preparation notes.

Always:
- include an observation hook, phenomenon, or concrete entry point when relevant
- support evidence-based questioning and misconception correction
- preserve scientific vocabulary, units, formulas, and notation accurately
- include diagram/demo/lab guidance when the session profile calls for it
- mention safety/caution notes when experiments, chemicals, electricity, heat, glassware, specimens, or equipment are relevant

Subject emphasis:
- Physics: stepwise reasoning, units, numerical method, graph/ray support, law-based explanation
- Chemistry: symbols, formula literacy, balanced-equation awareness, expected observations, particle/process reasoning
- Biology: labeled-structure guidance, process sequencing, function links, terminology precision

Do not force a lab block into a purely theory session.
