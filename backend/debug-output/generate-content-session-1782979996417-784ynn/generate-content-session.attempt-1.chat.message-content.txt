The oxygen atom is bonded to an sp3 hybridized carbon in a saturated chain.\n  *Example*: Ethanol (CH3CH2OH). Classification of Alcohols**\nTo predict reactivity, we classify alcohols based on the substitution level of the carbon holding the hydroxyl group:\n- **Primary (1°)**: The C-OH is attached to 0 or 1 other Carbon atom.\n  *Example*: Ethanol (CH3-**CH2**-OH).
