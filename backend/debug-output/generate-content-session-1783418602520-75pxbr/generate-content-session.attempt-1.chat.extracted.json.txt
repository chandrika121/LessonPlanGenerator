While less reactive than Mg, its oxide is still basic.\n\nNon-metals behave differently. Sulfur burns to form sulfur dioxide gas. This gas dissolves in water to form sulfurous acid, which turns blue litmus red. Thus, non-metal oxides are acidic.\n\nThis distinction helps us classify substances and predict their behavior in chemical reactions."
